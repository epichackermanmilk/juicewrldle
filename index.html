How to Play
1
Press ▶ Play to hear a short Juice WRLD audio clip. You get 1 play per guess — use it wisely!
2
Type a song name and select it from the dropdown, then click Guess. You have 7 guesses total.
3
Each guess (right or wrong) unlocks a hint token. Click 💡 Reveal Hint to spend a token and see a clue: Era → Length → Letters → First → Last → Producer.
4
Each wrong guess also extends the clip (+2s per guess). A new song drops every day — songs span released, unreleased, leaked, and recording session material.
5
Missed a day? Use 📅 History to play any past day. History replays don't affect your stats.
